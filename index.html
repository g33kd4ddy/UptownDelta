About Us

The Uptown Delta is a Charlotte, NC–based blues rock and soul band delivering high-energy, crowd-connecting live performances. Fronted by a dynamic female vocalist on keyboards, the band blends soulful vocals with gritty blues-rock tones. Their standout guitarist and primary songwriter drives the original material, while a locked-in rhythm section on drums and bass lays down deep pockets and dance-ready grooves. The Uptown Delta brings a polished yet raw sound that works equally well in clubs, outdoor stages, and festival settings—authentic blues soul with modern edge, strong musicianship, and undeniable feel.

The Uptown Delta is:

Vocals and Keys Sister Nancy
Guitar Ty Farley
Bass Joe Bronstein
Drums Brian Buchan
